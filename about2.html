Sustainability

●   OVERVIEW

We’re committed to creating high-performance hardscaping products for modern urban living that have minimal impact on our environment.

We’re proud to be the only grate manufacturer with four ticks under the SGBC Architectural Drain Covers category. Plus, we’ve earned other global accreditations for our commitment towards recycling, lowering our carbon footprint, and our waste, water and energy management.

Discover more
●   RECOGNITION

We strive to meet and raise industry benchmarks through resource and energy-saving initiatives.

SGBC

The four-ticks highest accolade is awarded based on a strict standard of green features. This accreditation enables architects to receive higher BCA Green Mark points.

LEED

Jonite is a member of the U.S. Green Building Council (USGBC) with the MR Credit 4: Recycled Content under the LEED® Green Building Rating System benchmark high-performance green buildings in the United States.

●   RESULTS

From innovation and production to delivery, we consider sustainable design every step of the way.

16x

By using 80% less steel, we've lowered our carbon footprint by 16x. We also use responsibly sourced cleaning agents and a solvent recycler, meaning even less waste and hazardous emissions.

30%

Every product is made using at least 30% recycled materials. Any surplus stone and steel that doesn't meet our strict manufacturing standards is reused as raw materials in future projects.

1%

Our composite mix replaces substances like quartz with safer raw materials. All our engineered stone grating products now feature less than 1% silica and are approved for use in Australia.

●   OUR PRINCIPLES

Made to matter

Made by nature.

All our decorative grates, covers and blocks are crafted using at least 95% natural aggregates—with up to 30% made from recycled content. The result: a more sustainable product—and a natural-looking, long-lasting finish.

Made to measure.

With endless customization options, we can create grates for any space and specification. The fact that many of our products are made to order means better results and less unnecessary waste.

Made to last.

Jonite products are designed to last a lifetime. Our stone composite can handle loads in the same way steel or cast iron can—but with zero rust or corrosion and a flawless finish for life.
